Objetivo: Business Architecture Milestone
At the end of the Elaboration phase, the main business architectural decisions have been taken and documented and the major risks have been addressed.
Descripción principal

Evaluation Criteria:

  • The Business Requirements are documented and stable.
  • High-level description of the to-be business has been documented.
  • The Business Architecture is stable.
  • Agreement on a refined Business Vision has been reached.
  • Major risks have been addressed and the stakeholders agreed with the resolutions.

Main Artifacts:

  • Business Vision - refined and reviewed by all stakeholders.
  • Business Goals - refined and reviewed by all stakeholders.
  • Business Glossary - refined.
  • Business Use-Case Model – stable and reviewed by all stakeholders.
  • Supplementary Business Requirements – stable and reviewed by all stakeholders.
  • Business Architecture Document - captures the “to-be” business architecture.
