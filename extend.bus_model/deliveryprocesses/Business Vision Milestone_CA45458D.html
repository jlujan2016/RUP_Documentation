Objetivo: Business Vision Milestone
At the end of the Inception phase, the business vision will capture the high-level objectives of the business modeling effort for the project.
Descripción principal

Evaluation Criteria:

  • The assessment of the organization under change has been completed.
  • High-level description of the current business has been documented.
  • Agreement on a preliminary set of objectives and expectations for the business-modeling effort.
  • Agreement on the scope of business modeling and on what modeling scenario should be followed.

Main Artifacts:

  • Target-Organization Assessment - completed
  • Business Vision - stable and reviewed by all stakeholders
  • Business Goals - preliminary and reviewed by all stakeholders
  • Business Glossary - preliminary
  • Business Architecture Document - captures the current description of the business
