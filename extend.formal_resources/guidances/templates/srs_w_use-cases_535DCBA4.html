Descripción principal

This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) template represents a form of the SRS where information from the Use-Case Model and the Supplementary Specifications are included by reference, as opposed to by value (i.e., duplicating).  This allows you to have a formal IEEE-compliant SRS without having to duplicate the information in multiple work products.
